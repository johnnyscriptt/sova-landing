5. Apple HealthKit

HealthKit data is handled with the highest level of privacy protection in compliance with Apple's guidelines.

Sova requests permission to write Mindful Minutes to your Apple Health app to record your affirmation listening sessions as mindfulness activities.

HealthKit Data Protections

  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising
  • HealthKit data is never sold to third parties
  • HealthKit data is never shared for marketing
  • You can revoke access at any time in Settings
